Sasa utacheza mchezo kwenye machine yako ambayo itakuwa inawakilisha vurugu. Kazi yako itakuwa ni kujilinda kutokana na kuumia. Lengo la mchezo liitakuwa kutumia mishale kujisongesha kwenye skrini yako ili kuzuia vitu vya hatari kukugonga. Utaanza kwa kuwa na afya kamili, ambayo ni ya thamani ya shilingi 50. Kila wakati unapo gongwa kama hujajizuia, inamaanisha utaumia, na utapoteza afya na kiasi cha pesa ulicho pewa. Mwisho wa sekunde 90, mchezo huu utakamilika na utalipwa pesa inayo lingana na afya uliyobakisha. Ukipoteza afya yako yote kabla ya sekunde 90 kufika, inamaanisha umeaga na utatolewa. Mchezo utaisha mapema na utapoteza shilingi zako 50 ulizoanza nazo.

Now you are going to play a game on the tablet which is meant to represent violence. Your job is to to protect yourself from getting hurt. The object of the game is to use arrows to move yourself around the screen in order to prevent dangerous items from hitting you. You start at full health, which is worth 50 Ksh. Every time an item hits you before you can escape it, you are injured, and you lose some health and some amount of Ksh. At the end of 90 seconds, the game will end and you will be paid the value in Ksh. of all the health you have remaining. If you lose all your health before 90 seconds are up, you die. The game will end early and you will lose all 50 Ksh. you started with.
