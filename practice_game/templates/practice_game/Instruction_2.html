Hapo chini utaona udhibiti wa mchezo huu.Unawakilishwa na laini unayoiona hapa chini ya skrini yako.Punde tu utakapo bonyeza "Start the Task", muda utaanza kuji hesabu na vitu hatari vitaanza kuanguka bila mpangilio wowote kutoka juu ya skrini. Unaweza kubonyeza mishale iliyo hapo chini ya skrini ili kujirudisha mbele na nyuma.Ili uepuka kitu cha hatari kukuguza, ni lazima uhakikishe hauko chini ya kitu hicho hatari wakati kitakapogusa chini ya skrini.Kumbuka, unapoteza pointi kila wakati kitu hatari kinakuguza.

Below you see the controls for the game. You are represented by the ﬂat object you see in the bottom of the screen. Once you click the “Start the Task” button, a countdown timer will start and dangerous items will start to fall randomly from the top of the screen. You can press the arrows below the screen to move yourself back and forth. To avoid a dangerous item, your must not be below the dangerous item when it touches the bottom of the screen. Remember, you lose points every time a dangerous object hits you.

If you are ready to play, please click the "Next" button. .
